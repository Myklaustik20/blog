First BLOG Post

Posted by Julio Skyy | April 20 2019

For many, money blogs are a much-needed source of guidance and inspiration. Personal finance can be complicated. There’s budgeting, saving, credit cards, investments, loans, retirement plans, insurance – that’s a lot to think about! And even before the COVID-19 pandemic struck, money was tight for many. According to the Federal Reserve, 39 percent of American adults wouldn’t be able to cover a $400 emergency with cash, savings, or a credit card paid off at the next statement. And the 2021 economic recession has only made things more difficult. As a result, it’s more vital than ever that we manage our money effectively. So, if you’re unsure how to pay off debt, which credit card to get, how to budget, or what you should invest in, these money blogs are full of information that can help. Let’s get into it. NerdWallet exists to help you “Make all the right money moves.” The site started in 2009 and has since grown into one of the best finance blogs online. With millions of monthly readers, this money blog provides detailed posts to help you make financial decisions about banking, credit cards, insurance, and more. Plus, NerdWallet also has plenty of financial tools to help you find the best accounts, loans, and credit cards.
